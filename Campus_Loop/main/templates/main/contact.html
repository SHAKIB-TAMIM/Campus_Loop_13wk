Frequently Asked Questions

How does the item exchange work?

Campus Loop connects buyers and sellers within the university community. List your items for sale and let interested buyers contact you to arrange payment and pickup/delivery.

Is there a fee for using Campus Loop?

No, Campus Loop is completely free for university students. We believe in making resource sharing and secondhand item exchange accessible to all students without any financial barriers.

Can I upload my own study materials?

Yes! We encourage students to share their notes, past exam papers, and other academic resources. Simply navigate to the Resources page and use the Upload Resource button.

How do I know if the items for sale are in good condition?

Sellers are required to provide accurate descriptions and photos of items, including their condition. You can also check seller ratings and reviews from previous transactions.

Can I use Campus Loop if I'm not a student?

Currently, Campus Loop is designed specifically for university students and requires verification with a valid university email address to register.
